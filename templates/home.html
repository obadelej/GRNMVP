473 MVP Track and Field Club

473 MVP track and field club started in 2020 as an offshoot of the Speedzone Athletics. MVP currently has 3 directors and an Executive board that runs the affairs of the club. Training is headed by Coach Albert Joseph with assistance from coaches Shana Perrotte and Obadele Joseph. Shana assist in the running events and Obadele in the throwing events. The club currently has over 60 athletes, most of them in the spints while a few does field events.

Our Vision

Providing an avenue for athletes to develop into top performers at the local, regional, and international competitions thus gaining scholarships to further their education so they have a career after track.

Our Vision

Empowering youths through athletics to bring about positive change and to develop world class athletes with a home base programme.
